Game Rules

The game has 2 players, playing in rounds

  • In each turn, a player rolls as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score
  • BUT, if the player rolls a 1 OR two 6's in a row, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLOBAL score (in the red box). After that, it's the next player's turn
  • The first player to reach 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game
